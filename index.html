Hi, I'm Natnael Daba

PhD Student in Computer Vision & Machine Learning

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona, working under the supervision of Prof. Abhijit Mahalanobis. My research focuses on computer vision and machine learning, with applications in visual localization, object detection, and novel view synthesis.

About Me

Prior to my PhD, I received my master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, with a concentration in machine learning.

My research interests span computer vision and machine learning, with particular focus on visual localization, object detection/classification, and novel view synthesis. I'm also exploring vision-language models, generative modeling, and diffusion models.
